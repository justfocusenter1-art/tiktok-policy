Privacy Policy

This application uses the TikTok API to analyze publicly available TikTok video performance data for analytics and research purposes.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The app only retrieves publicly available information such as video views, likes, and engagement statistics through the authorized TikTok API.

This data is used only to understand content performance trends and improve content strategy.

No automated posting, messaging, or user interaction is performed by this application.
